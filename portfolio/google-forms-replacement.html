Google Form Replacement

Google Apps Script

Summary

The client hit the limits of what Google Forms can offer. I developed a custom form management systems that enables admins to manage form questions and how they are ordered on the form. Included unlimited form branching and dynamic field pre-population.

Complete control over question metadata

Admins can add, edit, and remove questions. They can set different question types, as well as the answers to multiple-choice questions.

Admins can pull data from third-party systems based on a query parameter and pre-populate some questions based on that data.

Unlimited form branching

Admins can define as many branching as they need based on user answers. Each row in the sheet represents a possible flow.

Admins define the branching points and the sequence of questions that follows each branch.

Dynamic field evaluation

Admins can define custom conditionals that are calculated in real-time as users fill out the form.

For instance, admins can define some questions as required (or hidden) based on such conditionals.

Form preview

Admins can preview their changes using a Google Sheet sidebar that reflects their in the Metadata, Flows, or Conditionals sheets.

Once admins are happy with the results, they can publish their form changes.

Dynamic form experience

Users interact with the form via a Google Apps Script webapp. The webapp receives all necessary data from the GAS backend and renders the form accordingly.

When users submits their form responses, the webapp stores the data into a Google Sheet that admins can access and use.
